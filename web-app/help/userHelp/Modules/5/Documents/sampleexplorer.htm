Sample Explorer

This is a major new feature that lets you search for tissue and blood samples of interest.  Once you find the samples you want, you can perform tasks such as:

■    Finding Biobank IDs to locate the samples in the Biobank

■    Locating the study that produced the samples in the Dataset Explorer

■    Projecting sample data onto a heat map

Note:  In the current release, Sample Explorer heat maps can be based on Subset 1 definitions only.
