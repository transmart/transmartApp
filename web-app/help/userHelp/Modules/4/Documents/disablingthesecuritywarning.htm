Disabling the Security Warning

To prevent this security warning from appearing after you click the Pictor, ResNet, or GeneGO tabs, perform the steps below.

Keep in mind that disabling this security warning from appearing applies to all applications in which the security condition exists, not just tranSMART.

The following instructions apply to IE8, IE7, and IE6.

To disable the security warning:

1.  With tranSMART open in the IE browser, click the browser’s Tools menu, then click Internet Options.

2.  Click the Security tab.

3.  Click the Custom Level button.

4.  Scroll down to the Miscellaneous section (or try pressing the m key).

5.  Under Display mixed content, select the Enable radio button:

6.  Click OK.

7.  Click Yes to confirm the change.

8.  Click OK to close the Internet Options dialog.
