Sample Explorer

Sample Explorer lets you search for datasets of tested tissue and blood samples, within categories such as tissue type, pathology, and test type (such as gene expression or SNP).  You can also search only for samples that are included in the Biobank.

Once you find samples of interest, you can perform tasks such as:

■    Displaying data from one or more sample datasets in a visualization such as a heat map.

■    Linking back to the Dataset Explorer study for which the samples were collected.

■    Viewing reference information to help you locate samples in the Biobank.
